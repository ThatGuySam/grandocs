The key Update is located in the command area on the left of the key Clear.

![](/img/grandma2/img_update_keys_3_2-8d1d49.png)

_Location __Key __Update _

Pressing Update, enters the [Update keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_update/) into the [command line](/grandma2/key_widget_command_line/).

Pressing Update while a sequence or an effect is running, or while a preset is being called, plus when active different values are in the programmer, opens the pop-up Update. 

![](/img/grandma2/popup_update_3_2-3c91c8.png)
