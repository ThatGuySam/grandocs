The key Top is located in the command area on the right of the key Temp.

![](/img/grandma2/img_top_key_3_2-912b70.png)

_Location Key Top__ _

 

- Pressing Top enters the [Top keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_top/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Top

- Pressing Top Top enters the [Kill keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_kill/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Kill
