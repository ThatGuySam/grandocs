The key . is located in the command area on the left of the key If.

![](/img/grandma2/img_dot-key_v3-2-83a66a.png)

_Location k__ey __. \[dot] _

 

- Pressing . enters the . into the command line. The . is used as a separator to enter numbers with decimal fractions or to enter hierarchical object IDs.

\[Channel]> .

 

- Pressing . . executes the [Zero keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_zero/). The dimmer values of the selected fixtures are set to 0 %.\
   
- Pressing and holding ![ma](/img/grandma2/ma_1-290dfd.png) + . enters the [Default keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_default/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Default
