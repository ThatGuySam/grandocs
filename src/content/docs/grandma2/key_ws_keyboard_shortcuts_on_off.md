_Shortcut Button_

## Turn the keyboard shortcuts on

- Click Shortcut in the grandMA2 onPC or press **Insert** on your keyboard.

The keyboard shortcuts are turned on for the grandMA2 onPC. The shortcuts are displayed in brackets below the keys.

---

## Turn the keyboard shortcuts off

- Click Shortcut in the grandMA2 onPC or press **Insert** on your keyboard.

The keyboard shortcuts are turned off for the grandMA2 onPC.
