The key Fd Pg + is located on the left of the two 100 mm faders.

![](/img/grandma2/img_fader-page-plus-key_v3_2-c58013.png)

_Location k__ey __Fd Pg + _

 

Pressing Fd Pg + executes the [FaderPage keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_faderpage/). The fader page switches to the next page.

If you are on a channel page, press Fd Pg + to open the fader pages.
