It is possible to delete an imported image or video in two different ways. 

1. To delete single images:

- Press Del and then tap the image or video you would like to delete. 

2. To delete several images at once, type into the command line:

\[Channel] > Delete Image 14 Thru 24

Deletes images 14 to 24 in the image pool. 

**Important:**\
It is not possible to delete or edit predefined images or videos for that matter.
