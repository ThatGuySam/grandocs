The key Store is located in the command area on the left of the key

![ma](/img/grandma2/ma_1-290dfd.png).

![](/img/grandma2/img_store_key_3_2-12aa2a.png)

_Location __Key __Store _

 

- Pressing Store enters the [Store keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_store/) into the command line and changes the encoder bar to the [store encoder bar](/grandma2/key_ws_encoder_bar/).

 

\[Channel]> Store

 

![](/img/grandma2/img_enoder_bar_store_3_2-8b1d2e.png)

_Store Encoder Bar_

 

- Pressing and holding Store opens the pop-up Temporary Store Options.

 

![](/img/grandma2/popup_store_options_3_2-6b4d28.png)

_Pop-up Temporary Store Options _

 

- Pressing and holding ![ma](/img/grandma2/ma_1-290dfd.png) and Store enters the [Record keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_record/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Record
