The grandMA2 have three different functions that can help you work faster and more efficient.

They are called Worlds, Filters, and Masks.

[Worlds](/grandma2/key_wfm_what_are_worlds/) are used to limit the access to fixtures and attributes.

[Filters](/grandma2/key_wfm_what_are_filters/) are used to filter attribute values when doing different actions.

[Masks](/grandma2/key_wfm_what_are_masks/) are used to hide fixtures and attributes from your views.
