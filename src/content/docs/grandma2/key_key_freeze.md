The key Freeze is located in the command area on the right of the key Blind.

![](/img/grandma2/img_freeze-key_v3-2-8d62c8.png)

_Location k__ey __Freeze _

 

Pressing Freeze executes the [Freeze keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_freeze/) and turns on or off the freeze mode.

The command line stays unaffected.
