This section is about creating, editing and viewing the cues and sequences. This is some of the primary functions of a lighting console.

The topics here describe all the basic functions and operations regarding cues and sequences. Sequences are closely related to Executors. Taking a look at the [Executors section](/grandma2/key_exec/) might also be a good idea.

The more advanced options and functions for sequences are described in the [Advanced Sequence Functionality section](/grandma2/key_adv_seq/). For instance this section only describes the most used elements in the Sequence Executor Sheet - the advanced section describes the rest.
