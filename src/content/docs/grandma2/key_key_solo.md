The key Solo is located in the command area on the right of the key Highlt.

![](/img/grandma2/img_solo_key_3_2-0df634.png)

_Location __Key __Solo _

 

Pressing Solo executes the [Solo keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_solo/) and turns the solo mode on or off.

The command line stays unaffected.

To disable the Solo key function, see [User Settings](/grandma2/key_single_multi_systems_user_settings/).
