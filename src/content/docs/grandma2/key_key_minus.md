The key - is located on the right of the numeric keys in the command area.

![](/img/grandma2/img_minus_key_3_2-be8867.png)

_Location k__ey __\[Minus] - _

 

Pressing - once enters the [\[Minus\] - keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_minus/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> -

Pressing - twice subtracts 10 % dimmer value of the selected fixture or channel.
