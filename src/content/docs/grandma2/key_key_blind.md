The key Blind is located in the command area on the left of the key Freeze.

![](/img/grandma2/img_blind_key_3_2-a15b3e.png)

_Location k__ey __Blind _

 

Pressing Blind executes the [Blind keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_blind/). It toggles Blind on or off.

Pressing and holding Blind for 2 seconds executes [BlindEdit keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_blindedit/). It toggles BlindEdit on or off.

To change the function of the Blind key see [User Settings](/grandma2/key_single_multi_systems_user_settings/).
