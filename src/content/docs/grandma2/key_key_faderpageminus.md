The key Fd Pg - is located on the left of the 100 mm fader.

![](/img/grandma2/img_fader-page-minus-key_v3_2-cd60aa.png)

_Location k__ey __Fd Pg - _

 

Pressing Fd Pg - executes the [FaderPage keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_faderpage/).

If you are on a channel page, press Fd Pg - to open the fader pages.

If you press and hold the key Fd Pg - for 2 seconds, you will switch over to fader page 1.
