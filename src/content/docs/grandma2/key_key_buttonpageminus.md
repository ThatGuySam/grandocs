The key Bt Pg - is located left the 100 mm fader.

![](/img/grandma2/img_button_page_minus_key_3_2-6640fc.png)

_Location key Bt Pg -_

 

- Pressing Bt Pg - executes the [ButtonPage command](/grandma2/key_keyword_buttonpage/). That is: jumping to the previous ButtonPage. \
   
- If you press and hold Bt Pg -, you will automatically jump to the first ButtonPage.
