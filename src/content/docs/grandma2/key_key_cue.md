The key Cue is located in the command area on the right of the key Sequ.

![](/img/grandma2/img_cue_key_3_2-f3a51c.png)

_Location k__ey__ Cue _

 

- Pressing Cue enters the [Cue keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_cue/) into the command line.
