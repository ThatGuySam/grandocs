The key Assign is located in the command area on the right of the key Prvw.

![](/img/grandma2/img_assign_key_3_2-cf5671.png)

_Location k__ey __Assign _

 

- Pressing Assign enters [Assign keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_assign/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Assign

- Pressing Assign Assign enters [Label keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_label/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Label

- Pressing Assign Assign Assign enters [Appearance keyword](/grandma2/key_keyword_appearance/) into the command line.

\[Channel]> Appearance
