You can export fixtures to a USB drive or a folder.\
After the export it is possible to import these files to the console or the onPC software of both systems – dot2 and grandMA2.

## Export a Fixture to a USB Drive

1. Click **File**.
2. Click **Export to USB Drive**.\
   The window Export opens.

   ![](/img/utility/export_fixture_usb_drive_v1_3_1-90e6d5.png)

   _Export fixture to USB drive_
3. Select the USB device and click **Save to USB**.

The fixture file is exported to the selected USB drive.

The file is then saved into the subfolders "library" of dot2 and gma2.

---

## Export a Fixture to a Folder

1. Click **File**.
2. Click **Export to Folder**.
3. Choose the destination and click **Save To Folder**.

The file is exported and saved into the selected folder.
