Pressing the Exec key will open the [Executors Pool view](/dot2/key_viewitem_executorpool/). Here you can directly access all your executors. You can move cue lists around or make copies.

The key can also be used to specify a specific executor - If you are using it as a command ([Executor Command](/dot2/key_keyword_executor/)).

E.g. you want to run cue number 4 on executor number 6. here's the keys you need to press (using only keys):

Goto 4 Exec 6 Please

If you want to move executor 1 at executor 6:

Move Exec 1 At 6 Please

If you want to move the main executor (number 0.1.1) to executor 4:

Move Exec 0 . 1 . 1 At 4 Please

 

## Advanced Functionality

Pressing the ![ma](/img/dot2/ma-36da5c.png) key and then pressing the Exec key will give you the [Virtual playback view](/dot2/key_viewitem_virtualplayback/) on the right screen. Here you can have access to the playbacks/executors on the wings even tough you don't physically have the wings.
