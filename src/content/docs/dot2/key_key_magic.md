Pressing the Magic key will open the [Magic Speed view](/dot2/key_viewitem_speed/).

Here you have access to the 4 different timing and speed faders.

These 4 faders can also be assigned to physical fader executors - have a look at the [Speed](/dot2/key_key_speed/) and [Time](/dot2/key_key_time/) keys.
