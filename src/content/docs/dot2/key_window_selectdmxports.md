To go to the **Select DMX Ports** window, press Setup, tap Sessions, select **DMX Nodes** and tap and hold in a **universe cell**.

![](/img/dot2/dot2_viewsandwindows_selectdmxports01_1-0-63599b.png)

In this window, you select which universe range the Node4 should has as DMX output.

Choose between

- universe 1 - 4
- universe 5 - 8

To select a universe range, tap at the universe tile.

To leave the Select DMX Ports window, tap ![](/img/dot2/dot2_viewsandwindows_controlelements_titlebar08_1-0-5c7c42.png) in the title bar or press Esc on the console.
