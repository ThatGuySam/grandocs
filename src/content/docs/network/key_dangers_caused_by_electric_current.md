If the device or the power cable is damaged, there is a risk of death by an electrical shock.

Follow the safety instructions to avoid the risks by electric current.

- Do not use a damaged power cable.
- Do not open screwed covers from the body housing.
- Use the device only, if it is in a technical perfect condition.
- A broken device can be repaired only by an authorized serviced personal. Do not repair the device by your own.
- Do not make changes on the device, its components, and accessories.
- Do not expose the device to rain or moisture.
- Do not dive the device in water.
