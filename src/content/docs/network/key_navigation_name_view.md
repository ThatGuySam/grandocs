To open the name view on the display of the MA Network Switch, press the yellow menu button. 

![](/img/network/img_display-nav_name-view_v3-3-866fa6.png)

_Name View_

The column on the left of the display represents the back ports.

The column on the right is divided into three sections:

- SFP A
- SFP B
- Front Port
