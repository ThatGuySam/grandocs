The MA Network Switch is 1U high and will fit in most standard 19-inch racks.

1. Insert the switch into the 19-inch rack and secure with suitable screws.\
   Ensure the ventilation holes are not obstructed.
2. Connect the switch to the power supply.
3. Connect the cables.

The MA Network Switch is installed in a rack.
