To open the reset view, press and hold the menu button for about 5 seconds.

The reset view opens.

![](/img/network/reset-view_v3-1-4723fd.png)\
_Figure: Reset View_

To reset the switch to its [factory defaults](/network/key_factory_defaults/), follow the display instructions.\
For more information, see [Edit General Settings - Reset Settings](/network/key_reset_settings/).
