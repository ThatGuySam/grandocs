To open the LAG view on the display of the MA Network Switch, press the yellow menu button. 

![](/img/network/img_display-nav_lag-view_v3-3-ad0b8f.png)

_LAG View_

The column on the left and right is displayed in the same manner as in the [Name View](/network/key_navigation_name_view/).
