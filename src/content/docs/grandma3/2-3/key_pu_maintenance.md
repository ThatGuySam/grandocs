| ![](/img/grandma3/2-3/warning-b3b0cc.png) | **Warning:** The grandMA3 processing unit has an air intake filter on the front.  Do not wash the filter. Clean it using a vacuum cleaner only. |

- Disconnect power supply before cleaning; 
- Clean the surface with dry cloth only; 
- Do not use liquid cleanser or compressed air; 
- Do not use solvent; 
- Check if the ventilation holes are blocked; 
- Depending on the environment, we recommend to professionally clean the device every 2 to 5 years.
