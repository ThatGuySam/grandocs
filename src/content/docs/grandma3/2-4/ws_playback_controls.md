The Playback Controls menu shows an on-screen version of the physical faders of a console and/or wings.

To open Playback Controls, tap ![](/img/grandma3/2-4/icon_playback_15_v1-9-fe46cd.png) in the control bar on the left of screens 1 to 7 or F5 on a keyboard.

For more information on the functionality of executors, see [Executors](/grandma3/2-4/executor/).
