MA Lighting offers the perfect solutions for getting started with. Simply download the grandMA3 onPC software and connect to any of the grandMA3 onPC products to unlock parameters.

The amount of DMX outputs varies with each grandMA3 onPC product but all of them unlock parameters for the full grandMA3 software functionality. There is no compromise in feature set and the range of products allow you to make the right choice for your budget or individual needs.

You have a wide variety of options for onPC solutions: the grandMA3 onPC command wing, which offers the ultimate grandMA3 mobile solution, the grandMA3 onPC command wing XT, featuring a built-in MA mother board, the grandMA3 onPC xPort Nodes, which offer the most cost-effective DMX output solution for software only show control, the grandMA3 onPC DMX-key and the grandMA3 onPC DMX-key starter, which offer two DMX outputs in the most compact solution, and the grandMA3 onPC fader wing, which gives you additional faders in your grandMA3 onPC system.
