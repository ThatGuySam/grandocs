## Multi Select

To select multiple cells in a sheet, hold Ctrl and tap or click each cell.

##

## Clean Start

To perform a clean start, hold Ctrl when the mode selection pop-up appears during the boot or reboot process and tap the desired mode. For more information about clean start, see [Clean Start](/grandma3/2-4/ts_clean_start/).

## Location

Ctrl is located in two places on the bottom row of the alpha-numeric keyboard.
