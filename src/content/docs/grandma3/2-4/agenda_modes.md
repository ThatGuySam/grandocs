Open an agenda window, see [Add Windows](/grandma3/2-4/wvm_add_window/). From the add window dialog, tap Tools, and then tap Agenda Viewer.

The agenda window can display data in five view modes: Sheet, Year, Month, Week, and Day.

To change the view, tap View Mode in the title bar.
