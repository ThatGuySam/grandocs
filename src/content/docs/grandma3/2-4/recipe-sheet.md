The recipe editor with its multiple columns is part of recipes in [cue parts](/grandma3/2-4/cue_recipe/), [presets](/grandma3/2-4/presets_recipes/), and the [Recipe Editor window](/grandma3/2-4/recipe-editor-window/). Besides minor differences, the recipe editor covers the same information in all three places.

For settings that are mutually exclusive for the recipe editor window, read [Recipe Editor window](/grandma3/2-4/recipe-editor-window/). 

To create a new standard or phaser recipe, tap and hold the cell **New Recipe**, or select the cell and tap + on the top left side. A pop-up opens: 

![](/img/grandma3/2-4/select_recipe_pop-up_2-4-d81c5f.png)

Alternatively, you can add recipes tapping Add Standard Recipe and Add Phaser Recipe on the right side of the context area. For more information on the context area, read [below](/grandma3/2-4/recipe-sheet/#h2__1449911386).
