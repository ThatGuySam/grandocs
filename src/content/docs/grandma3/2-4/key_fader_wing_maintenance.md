|                                                  |                                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![](/img/grandma3/2-4/important_gray-89eed8.png) | **Important:**                                                                                                       |
|                                                  | The intervals between maintenance may differ depending on the frequency of transport and the operational environment |

- Disconnect the power supply before cleaning; 
- Clean the surface with dry cloth only; 
- Do not use liquid cleanser or compressed air; 
- Do not use solvent; 
- Depending on the environment, we recommend to professionally clean the device every 2 to 5 years.
