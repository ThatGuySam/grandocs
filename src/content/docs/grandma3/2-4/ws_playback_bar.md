The playback bar displays the labels of the objects that are currently assigned to the corresponding executors.

The bar includes:

- The playback status of each object

- The controls assigned to the executor keys, faders, and knobs

- In some cases:

  - [Master Area](/grandma3/2-4/do_control_master/)
  - [Custom Area](/grandma3/2-4/do_control_custom/)
  - Page navigation controls
  - Xkeys
