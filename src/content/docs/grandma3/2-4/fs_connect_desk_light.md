The grandMA3 comes with one or two desk lights, depending on the model. For more information see, [delivery contents](/grandma3/2-4/fs_scope_delivery/).

Connect the 4-pin XLR connector to the desk light connectors on the rear panel. The desk lights are connected. 

To adjust the light level, see the [Desk Lights](/grandma3/2-4/si_desklights/) topic.
