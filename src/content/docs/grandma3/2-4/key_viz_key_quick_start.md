### Start-Up

1. Unpack the device.
2. Remove the packaging and cushioning material. 
3. Connect the grandMA3 viz-key via one of the USB-C cables to a PC or laptop with installed grandMA3 onPC software.
4. To power off the grandMA3 viz-key, disconnect the USB-C cable.
5. To update your grandMA3 device, download the latest software version on [www.malighting.com](https://help.malighting.com/grandMA3/2.4/HTML/www.malighting.com).\
   For more information see [Connect grandMA3 viz-key](/grandma3/2-4/fs_connect_viz_key/) in the online manual.
