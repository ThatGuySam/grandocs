Stopwatches are the default timer. Learn more in the [Timers topic](/grandma3/2-4/timers/).

They count time up from 0 and do not have a practical upper limit.

The stopwatch timer has a stopwatch icon (![](/img/grandma3/2-4/icon_stopwatch_15_v1-9-6f6b6a.png)) in the upper right corner of the pool object.

A stopwatch will run for several days if it is not stopped or paused.

Stopwatches do not have any special settings.
