		<h1>Connect Desk Lamps</h1>


		<p>The console comes with two MA LED desk lamps.</p>

<ul>
	<li>Connect the 4 pin XLR connector to the desk lamp connectors on the rear panel.</li>
</ul>

<p>The desk lamps are connected.</p>

<figure class="caption"><img alt="" src="/Media/Image/img_grandma2_desklampconnector_3_2.png">
<figcaption><em>grandMA2&nbsp;full-size desk lamp connectors</em></figcaption>
</figure>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>To adjust the intensity of the desk lamp see <a href="/Topic/3ddb3c0d-823f-4aab-9065-7c41c1ba1278">adjust desk lights</a>.</p>
