		<h1>Up Key</h1>


		<p>The key&nbsp;<span class="hardkey">Up</span>&nbsp;is located in the command area above the key&nbsp;<span class="hardkey">Set</span>.</p>

<figure class="caption"><img alt="" src="/Media/Image/img_up_key_3_2.png">
<figcaption><em>Location&nbsp;</em><em>Key&nbsp;</em><em>Up&nbsp;</em></figcaption>
</figure>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
	<li>If you press the key&nbsp;<span class="hardkey">Up</span>, the scroll bar of the window or pop-up, in which the focus is set, moves up.</li>
	<li>If you press and hold <span class="hardkey"><img alt="ma" src="/Media/Mlg/ma_1.png"></span> +&nbsp;<span class="hardkey">Up</span>, the monitor wing of the console moves up.</li>
</ul>
